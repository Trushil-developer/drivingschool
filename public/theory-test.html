Frequently Asked Questions

How many questions are in the RTO theory test?

The RTO Learner Licence theory test has 15 multiple-choice questions. Each question has 3 options (A, B, C). You get 48 seconds per question and need at least 9 correct answers to pass.

What topics are covered?

Questions cover Traffic Rules, Road Signs, Vehicle Knowledge, First Aid, Road Safety, and Motor Vehicle Act regulations. All questions are based on the official RTO syllabus for LMV category.

Is this the same as the real RTO exam?

This mock test follows the same format, time limit and pass criteria as the actual computer-based test at the RTO office. Questions are sourced from the official RTO question bank to give you the most realistic preparation.

Do I need to create an account?

Just enter your email and verify with a one-time code. No password needed. Your mock test results and practice progress are automatically saved to your account.
